POCHERONG TILAPIA

INGREDIENTS

1/2 cup cooking oil
3 pcs ripe plantains
2 pcs potatoes
1 kg fish
1 pc onion
6 cloves garlic
2 pcs tomatoes
4 tbsp fish sauce, or salt
6 cups water
Pepper
Cabbage
Tomato sauce
Sugar to taste

INSTRUCTION

Start off by frying bananas and potatoes in a pan, set them aside.
Using the same oil, fry the fish then set aside.
Using a pan sauté onion, garlic, and tomatoes.
Add fish sauce.
Add water then wait for the mixture to boil.
Once the mixture is boiling, add tomato sauce then mix well.
Put in a little sugar and pepper to taste.
Add cabbage and pechay then simmer for 5 minutes (until veggies are soft).
Add in fried bananas and potatoes next. Mix well then simmer for 3 minutes.
Add the fried fish then mix to coat fish with sauce, simmer for a minute just to let the fish absorb the sauce then serve it.
