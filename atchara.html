ATCHARA

INGREDIENTS

1 large unripe green papaya, peeled, seeded and shredded
2 tablespoons + 1 teaspoon salt
1-1/2 cups white vinegar
1 cup sugar
1 thumb-size ginger 
1 small onion 
3 to 4 cloves garlic 
1 tablespoon peppercorns
1/2 small red bell pepper seeded
1/2 small green pepper seeded
1 large carrot
1/4 cup raisins

INSTRUCTION

In a bowl, combine shredded papaya and 2 tablespoons of the salt.
Allow to sit for about 1 hour or until papaya begins to release liquid.
Place papaya in a cheesecloth and squeeze firmly to dispel juices.
In a sauce pot over medium heat, combine vinegar, sugar and the remaining 1 teaspoon of salt.
Bring to a simmer and cook, stirring occasionally, for about 3 to 5 minutes or until sugar and salt are dissolved.
Add ginger, onions, garlic and peppercorns. Continue to cook for about 2 to 3 minutes.
In a bowl, combine papaya, bell peppers, carrots and raisins, add pickling solution and toss gently to combine.
In a sterilized jar, transfer atchara and close with tight-fitting lid.
Refrigerate for 1 to 2 days to allow flavors to develop before serving.
