INGREDIENTS

1/2 lb. honeycomb tripe
1/2 lb. blanket tripe
1/2 lb. beef heart
1/4 lb. small intestine
1/2 lb. beef tendon
2 large red onions chopped
3 pieces Thai chili
2 quarts rice wash
1/2 cup annatto seeds diluted in water
1 tablespoon salt
1 teaspoon crushed whole peppercorn
2 teaspoons garlic powder
1 beef bouillon
1/2 cup chopped green onions
2 quarts water

INSTRUCTION

Pour water in a large cooking pot and let it boil.
Add all the beef innard, cover and cook in medium heat.
Discard the water and pour rich washing into the pot, let boil.
Add salt, crushed peppercorn, garlic powder, onion, chili, and annatto water and stir.
Add beef bouillon, cover and cook in low heat for 4 hours.
Remove the innards from the cooking pot.
Slice into serving pieces and Arrange in a bowl.
Pour hot broth in the bowl and top with chop green onions and chili.
Serve with a dipping sauce composed of chopped onion, soy sauce, calamansi, and chili.
