SINAING NA TULINGAN

INGREDIENT

1 lb Tulingan (bullet tuna)
1/2 cup Vinegar
1/2 tbsp Salt
2 thumbs Ginger (julienned)
1/2 tsp Cracked black pepper
1 cup Water
4 pcs Long green chilies
1 tbsp Cooking oil

INSTRUCTION

Clean the fish by removing the innards, gills and tail.
Put in a casserole.
Put-in ginger and green chilies.
Pour water and vinegar, and season with salt and pepper.
Cook for about 35 minutes.
Add cooking oil and simmer for another 5
