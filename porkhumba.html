PORK HUMBA

INGREDIENTS

1 pound pork
5 cloves garlic
3 tablespoons soy sauce
2 tablespoons vinegar
3 tablespoons brown sugar
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
2 tablespoons oil
3 pieces star anise
1/2 cup water
1/4 teaspoon salt

INSTRUCTION

Marinate pork in soy sauce, garlic, vinegar, and ground pepper salt in a bowl.
Heat a large pan on the stove then add the marinated pork stir until it brown.
Add the star anise and brown sugar.
Add water then cover the pan and let boil on medium for 25 minutes.
Transfer to a serving plate and enjoy.
