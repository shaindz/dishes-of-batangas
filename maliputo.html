SINIGANG NA MALIPUTO

INGREDIENTS

1 whole Maliputo, 350 grams
1 cup fresh tamarind
2 medium size tomato, quartered
2 medium size onions, quartered
1 cup sliced radish
1 cup string beans
5 okra, sliced
1 bundle kangkong
4 pieces siling haba
Salt and pepper

INSTRUCTION

Score both sides of the maliputo, season with salt and pepper.
Boil 8-10 cups of water in large pot. Add onion, tomato and tamarind fruit and simmer.
Add the maliputo fish.
Add vegetables and siling haba except kangkong. Cook for 2-3 minutes.
Let the ingredients simmer until half-cooked.
Bring the stock to a boil and add the kangkong and serve with fish sauce.
