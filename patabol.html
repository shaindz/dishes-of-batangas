PATABOL

INGREDIENTS

2 3/4 cups glutinous rice flour
1 cup water
1 cup young coconut or macapuno, shredded
2 cups coconut cream
1/2 cup water
1/4 tsp iodized salt
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup muscovado sugar

INSTRUCTION

Prepare the glutinous rice flour in a container.
Add 2 cups of water and mixt to make dough.
Use your hand to make smooth dough.
Divide the dough in your desire equal parts.
Flatten the dough and add your macapuno and make it oblong.
Prepare the pan, pour in 1 cup of coconut cream and add 1 cup of water.
Add brown sugar, ½ muscavado sugar, ¼ tsp of salt and let it boil.
Put the stuff dough and continue to cook.
Put on the plate and serve it.
