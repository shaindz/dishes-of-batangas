GINATAANG LANGKA

INGREDIENTS

1/2 kg. Unripened Jackfruit
2 cups Coconut Milk
1 cup Coconut Cream
5 cloves Garlic, minced
1 medium Onion, diced
1 tablespoon Shrimp Paste
4 cloves Garlic, minced
1 medium Onion, chopped
2–3 Long Chili Peppers
Salt and Pepper
Cooking Oil

INSTRUCTION

1.In a Pan, saute the garlic and onion.
2.Mix in the shrimp paste and jackfruit and cook for 1-2 minutes.
3.Add the coconut milk and bring to a boil then turn the heat down to simmer until the vegetable is slightly tender, about 10 – 15 minutes.
4.Stir in the coconut cream to thicken the sauce, together with the long chili peppers.
5.Season with salt (as needed) and ground pepper to taste.
6.Adjust the ingredients according to taste.
7.Best Served with Rice
